- **Simple Deployment**: Single Next.js application architecture

## ðŸ”„ **RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION**

### **Business Risks**

#### **High-Risk Items**
1. **Data Provider Dependencies**
   - Risk: Loss of key data sources
   - Mitigation: Multiple redundant providers, contractual agreements

2. **Competitive Response**
   - Risk: Major players copying unique features
   - Mitigation: Patent filing, rapid innovation, network effects

3. **Regulatory Changes**
   - Risk: Data access restrictions or compliance requirements
   - Mitigation: Legal monitoring, compliance frameworks, lobbying

#### **Medium-Risk Items**
1. **Technical Scalability**
   - Risk: Platform cannot handle user growth
   - Mitigation: Load testing, auto-scaling architecture, performance monitoring

2. **Customer Concentration**
   - Risk: Over-dependence on large customers
   - Mitigation: Diversified customer base, long-term contracts

### **Mitigation Strategies**
- **Insurance**: Professional liability and cyber security coverage
- **Legal**: Strong IP protection and data agreements
- **Financial**: 6-month cash runway maintenance
- **Technical**: Redundant systems and disaster recovery plans
